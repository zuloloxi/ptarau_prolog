Historical Stock Market Prolog Database Generator. How it works?

Just fill in a stock ticker and click on the link to download and
save the resulting Prolog database file. A Jinni Server Agent will
find on the net the historical data for the stock, figure out the 
day of the week for each entry and convert it to a Prolog file.
That can take a few seconds, unless someone has already created the
file. Data is adjusted for stock splits and goes back to 1990. For
instance, assuming you chose ticker ibm, clauses look like this:

ibm(Date,DayOfWeek,Open,High,Low,Close,Volume,AdjustedClose).

You can also download this 4MB Prolog database about stocks
listed on the Nasdaq, Nyse and Amex markets, with clauses of the form:

stock(Ticker,Market,Number,CompName,MarketCapInThousandDollars,Description).
